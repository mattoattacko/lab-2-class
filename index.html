Bio

Howzit brah!? My name is Matt and, as you saw in my game, I was born and raised in Hawaii. I moved to Washington in December of 2015 to pursue a doctorate in physical therapy. Getting into the program of my choice ended up taking longer than I had anticipated, so I decided to pursue other options.

I work in outpatient physical therapy in Bellevue, where we see a lot of clients from the big tech companies. The programmers always seemed to enjoy their job, so I spoke with a few of them and was recommended Code Fellows. I'm really enjoying it so far, and hope it leads to an interesting and challenging career.
